Overview

Purpose

This website is aimed at establishing a strong online presence for our company and leveraging the power of the internet for information dissemination, booking and reservation, marketing and promotion, and customer engagement.This site will be of significant benefits to our rafting company in that, it will increase brand visibility, streamline operations, enhance credibility, cost-effective marketing, and be a good source of data collection.

Audience

We designed this site and business with adventure enthusiasts and outdoor adventurers in mind.These people crave thrilling outdoor experiences (adventure seekers), they appreciate natural beauty and are drawn to scenic location (nature lovers), they enjoy exploring new place and love travelling (travel enthusiasts), we also considered family and group planners..

The website content would feature vivid description of our rafting tours, scenic locations, using natural and vibrant colors. The fonts will be clean and easy-to-read; the imagery will be of high quality and the layout would be user-friendly which will encourage the site visitor to take the next step in their adventure.
